Short Biography:

Tyler Chang is a postdoc in the MCS Division at Argonne. Prior to joining Argonne, he was the Cunningham doctoral fellow at Virginia Tech, where he completed his Ph.D. in Computer Science, with a specialization in numerical analysis. His research interests include approximation theory, blackbox optimization, computational geometry, and open-source software.
